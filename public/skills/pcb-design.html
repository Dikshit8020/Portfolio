01 / Origin

How I Learned PCB Design

Started with EasyEDA for simple breakout boards. Moved to KiCad when I needed multi-layer designs and custom footprints. The Li-Fi project was the crucible — designing a precision analog TIA on a 2-layer PCB where stray capacitance on traces directly affects signal quality forced me to learn proper ground plane management, star-point grounding, and trace impedance control.

LTspice became essential for validating analog designs before ordering PCBs. Simulating the IRFZ44N laser driver circuit caught a voltage spike issue that would have destroyed the laser diode.
