From Mentoring to Mastering

As Head of C.O.R.E. robotics club, I've guided 150+ students through projects from line-followers to articulated arms. Teaching kinematics to first-year students forced me to deeply understand DH parameters, workspace analysis, and singularity avoidance.

The TIFAN 2025 transplanter project was the real test — building a 6-DOF arm that must move 40 times per minute with sub-millimetre repeatability while coordinating pick, place, and tray advancement simultaneously.
